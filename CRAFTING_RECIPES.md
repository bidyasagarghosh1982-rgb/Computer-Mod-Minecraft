## 🎮 Usage in Survival

1. Gather raw materials (Iron, Gold, Copper, etc.)
2. Craft individual components (GPU, CPU, RAM, etc.)
3. Use **Assembly Station** to combine components
4. Create one of 4 pre-built PC configurations
5. Place in your base for display or decoration!

**Crafting times:** Each component takes ~10-30 seconds to craft at a workbench.
**Durability:** All components are decorative/collectible (no durability loss).
